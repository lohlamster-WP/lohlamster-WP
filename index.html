How Webtek Started

Wayne founded Webtek Sdn Bhd after observing that many SMEs struggled with manual processes and inefficient workflows. He set out to create digital solutions that reduce repetitive tasks, optimize operations, and let business owners focus on what matters — growth.

ABOUT ME

Turning Technology into Business Impact

Wayne Poon is a Malaysian technology entrepreneur and the Founder & CEO of Webtek Sdn Bhd — a technology company specialising in mobile applications, software development, AI automation, and digital platform solutions.

Webtek helps businesses transform digitally by building scalable, intelligent, and user-friendly systems that improve efficiency, reduce operational costs, and create better customer experiences.

Wayne leads the company with a belief that technology should be accessible, efficient, and cost-effective — empowering businesses and entrepreneurs to compete confidently in an increasingly digital marketplace.

Short Bio
